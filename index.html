Thalia Gotcha

Campus

Start: 22-04-2022

End: 29-04-2022

€1,00

Thalia gotcha game with a twist, every participant gets assigned a watergun and a target (another participant). The goal is to assassinate your target with this watergun. Instead of being permanently eliminated after getting killed, the dead will be resurrected daily. After every successful kill, you recieve some points + a new target. More kills = more points! The person with the most points at the end of the week recieves a prize. More rules will follow, but in principle this game can be played at any place throughout the whole lustrum week, so keep you guard up!

Programming Contest

Giga Byte

26-04-2022

16:00 - 21:00

Free

Get ready and prepare yourself for the Thalia Lustrum Programming Contest! During this event, you will be a member of a team of 2 or 3 people, with whom you are going to try to solve a very fun and challenging task! This task consists of creating a bot that will attempt to win a game. After every team creates their own bot, we will host a tournament where the bots get to 'fight' other bots!
